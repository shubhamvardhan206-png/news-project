Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

NewsPortal ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process personal information in connection with our websites, mobile applications, and other online services (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Registration & Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, password, phone number, and profile information.
  • Subscription & Payment Information: We collect billing address, payment method details, transaction history, and subscription preferences.
  • Communications: When you contact us, we collect the content of your messages, attachments, and contact information.
  • Survey & Feedback: We may collect information from surveys, polls, and feedback forms you complete.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device & Browser Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, articles read, time spent, search queries, and interaction patterns.
  • Location Information: Approximate location based on IP address (we do not collect precise location without your consent).
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to track your activity.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • 📰 Deliver Services: Provide, maintain, and improve our Services.
  • 👤 User Accounts: Create and manage your account, verify identity, and authenticate users.
  • 💳 Payments: Process transactions, manage subscriptions, and prevent fraud.
  • 📧 Communications: Send newsletters, updates, alerts, and respond to your inquiries.
  • 🎯 Personalization: Customize content recommendations based on your preferences and behavior.
  • 📊 Analytics: Understand user behavior, improve user experience, and conduct research.
  • 🔒 Security: Detect fraud, prevent unauthorized access, and comply with legal obligations.
  • 📢 Marketing: Send promotional content (with your opt-in consent).

3. How We Share Your Information

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who process data on our behalf (payment processors, email services, analytics providers).
  • Business Partners: With advertising partners and content providers (with your consent).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy.
  • Public Information: User-generated content like comments and articles may be publicly visible.

⚠️ Important: We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Your Rights & Choices

4.1 Access & Portability

You have the right to access your personal information and request a copy of the data we hold about you.

4.2 Correction & Deletion

You can update or delete your account information anytime in your account settings or by contacting us.

4.3 Email Preferences

You can opt-out of promotional emails using the unsubscribe link in every email or adjusting preferences in your account.

4.4 Cookie Management

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may limit functionality of our Services.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption (SSL/TLS), secure authentication, regular security audits, and restricted access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords and never share your login credentials.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide Services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. You can request deletion of your account anytime, though we may retain certain information as required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information promptly.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via email or prominent notice on our website. Continued use of our Services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
