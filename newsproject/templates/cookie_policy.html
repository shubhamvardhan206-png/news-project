Cookie Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They contain information that can be retrieved later by the website server to remember your preferences and actions.

2. How We Use Cookies

NewsPortal uses cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

  • User authentication and session management
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Basic website functionality

Performance Cookies

  • Website analytics and usage statistics
  • Performance monitoring and optimization
  • Error tracking and debugging

Functional Cookies

  • Remembering user preferences and settings
  • Personalizing user experience
  • Storing language preferences

Marketing Cookies

  • Displaying relevant advertisements
  • Tracking advertising campaign effectiveness
  • Understanding user interests and behavior

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Session Cookies

These cookies exist only during your browsing session and are deleted when you close your browser. They are used to maintain your login status and shopping cart contents.

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a specified period even after you close your browser. They help us recognize you on your next visit and remember your preferences.

First-Party Cookies

These are cookies set by NewsPortal directly to provide functionality and better user experience.

Third-Party Cookies

These are cookies set by third-party services (e.g., Google Analytics, advertising networks) to track behavior and deliver personalized content. We do not control these cookies directly.

4. How to Manage Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent
  • Opt-Out Tools: You can use opt-out tools provided by advertising networks
  • Do Not Track: You can set your browser to send a "Do Not Track" signal

Note: If you disable cookies, some features of NewsPortal may not work properly, and your user experience may be affected.

5. Cookie Consent

When you first visit NewsPortal, we display a cookie consent banner. By clicking "Accept" or continuing to browse, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Google Analytics: For website traffic analysis and user behavior tracking
  • Google Ads: For displaying targeted advertisements
  • Social Media Platforms: For social sharing and integration features
  • Payment Processors: For handling subscription and payment transactions

7. Data Retention

Cookies are retained for different periods depending on their type:

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Up to 2 years from the date of creation
  • Analytics cookies: Up to 26 months

8. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. Your continued use of NewsPortal indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
