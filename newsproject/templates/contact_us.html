Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a news tip?

You can submit news tips via email at tips@newsportal.com or use the contact form above. We investigate all credible tips and follow up with sources.

Do you accept guest articles?

Yes! We welcome guest contributions from experts and experienced writers. Please email us with your article proposal at info@newsportal.com.

How can I report an error or inaccuracy?

If you find an error in our reporting, please let us know immediately at corrections@newsportal.com. We take accuracy seriously and will investigate promptly.

What is your comment policy?

We welcome thoughtful comments on our articles. However, we reserve the right to remove comments that are offensive, defamatory, or spam.
